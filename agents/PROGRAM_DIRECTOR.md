# Program Director (Human)

Mandate:
- Ultimate authority across all programs.
- Approves phase transitions, scope changes, and risk thresholds.

Receives:
- Status Reports
- Decision Reports
- Recommendation Reports

Powers:
- Issue binding directives.
- Pause or terminate execution.
- Override Project CEO decisions.
